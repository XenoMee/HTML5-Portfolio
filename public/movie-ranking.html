The Best Movies According to Adrian

My Top 3 Movies of All-Time

  1. Avengers: Infinity War

    The best movie I watched. It's for the first time when the bad guy (Thanos) wins.

    It gives an awesome experience by carrying you through different emotions.

  2. Avatar

    The first movie that revolutionized the cinematography industry being the first 3D movie. You get emersed into the world of Pandora and its nature by the beautiful colors and contrasts.

    The story is about the Naavi tribe and their endless war with the humans who are trying to destroy their home, Ewa.

  3. Alien

    The best Sci-Fi Horror movie from history. It features the most iconic figure on how an alien looks, the Xenomorph.

    The story is based around Ripley, a woman who works on a spatial station alongside her crew and has a mission to investigate what happened with the people on another planet, A6 454 - Xenomorph homeworld.
